If you believe you have found a bug and would like to report it, please feel free to report it as an "issue" on the project's GitHub page. When submitting an issue report, please copy the link that appears in the paragraph below the "submit" button on the calculator, in order to provide me with inputs that you provided to the calculator.

Finally, if you simply have a question about why the calculator is recommending a particular filing date, you may want to start a conversation on the Bogleheads Forum. There are many forum members who are familiar with the calculator.
