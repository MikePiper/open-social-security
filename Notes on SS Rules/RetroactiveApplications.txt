Retirement benefits can be retroactive up to 6 months, but not prior to FRA

Spousal benefits can be retroactive up to 6 months, but not prior to FRA.
    *12 months instead of 6 months, if other person is disabled (i.e., if person on whose work record you are receiving spousal benefits is disabled)

Child benefits can be retraoctive up to 6 months (12 months if parent is disabled)
